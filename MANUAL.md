    add-contact <name> <address> <phone> <email> <birthday>

    Add contact.
        name - Contact Name
        address - Contact Address
            The address must contain from 5 to 100 characters.
            And shouldn't contain special characters: !@#$%^&*()
        phone - 
        email - 
        birthday -
